Screening Survey


This survey takes in a person's age, weight, height, and any other health information that could further impact that person's susceptability to certain diiseases (like COVID-19). I used the Apple Weather App as inspiration to visualize how susceptible a person is to getting sick based of their location and health. For example if a person is in the green, it means that they are relatively safe but the closer it gets to the red area, they might be putting themselves in danger of catching whatever is in the air.
