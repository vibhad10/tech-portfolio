Opportunity


Expanding on my expertise in product design I have create a project that could become a reality in the future. As you scroll down, you will see that I have conducted User Research to finalize my design and have attached pictures of my wireframs and completed design. This project is meant for critique and additional edits so please contact me with an inspired ideas! Let's start off with what was the problem and how I solved it.


The Problem


Scrolling through 100's of instagram posts is boring. No offense to all the people who took awesome pictures on their summer trip to Europe, but I only wanted to see the main accoutns I follow today. After interviewing people around my age at UC Berkeley, I found that this want was universal and I should do something about it. Or at least make a design for it. Here is the before:

The Final Product


Instagram users would be able to access posts from their favorite singers, newsletters, friends, and many others in a click of a button.
